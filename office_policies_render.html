Office Policies

 Fieldworks, LLC.

Position: The purpose of the quality control position is to identify suspicious documents and to maintain a clear and accurate accounting of every document.

Performance:

  • Minimum Standard: You must meet minimum signature requirements for each shift worked within the pay period. You must complete the daily visual review, PDF scanning, assist with check out, and send to QC process for each page every day.

Requirements:

  • You must report any suspicious documents or behavior by other staff to your supervisor immediately.
  • You must adhere to all local, state, and US laws, as well as all FieldWorks Office and Staff policies.

Pay: Office quality control pay is $16.00 per hour.
