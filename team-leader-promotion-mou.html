Team Leader Promotion Memorandum of Understanding

Fieldworks, LLC.

You have been promoted to Team Leader, effective the beginning of your next shift.

As a Team Leader, you will receive a pay increase as shown on the Office Policies.

In addition to your normal duties as outlined in your training and the FieldWorks Staff and Office Policies, you may now have other additional responsibilities during your work shift. These responsibilities may include but are not limited to:

  • Daily Skills Sessions
  • Crew Calls
  • Crew Huddle
  • Vehicle Rides
  • Drop Offs and Pickups
  • Managing Crew Sheets
  • Day 1 in Field Training
  • Mid Shift Check-Ins
  • Advanced Trainings
  • Troubleshooting Field Calls
  • Office duties within shift time
  • Out-of-Office Social Planning

On days that you are responsible for training a Day 1 because these extra responsibilities may reduce the amount of time you are able to spend canvassing, you will be held to a reduced standard for that day, as specified in the Office Policies.

I understand that as a Team Leader, I am not permitted to:

  • Get on CHRIS or OneDrive
    • Check people in
    • Check people out
  • Cut Turf or Assign Sites
  • Pick My Own Crew Members
  • Assign Crews
  • Create Driving Directions
  • Do Debriefs
  • Work Extra Hours Outside of the Shift (Except Weekly Meeting & Initial Training)
  • Volunteer (Work Off the Clock)
