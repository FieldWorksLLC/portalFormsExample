Driver Promotion Memorandum of Understanding

Fieldworks, LLC.

You have been promoted to a Driver and are eligible to start driving for us starting your next shift.

As a Driver, you will receive an additional $10 in pay per shift for every shift that you drive.

As a reminder, FieldWorks takes safe driving very seriously. SAFETY IS YOUR TOP PRIORITY AS A DRIVER.

In addition to your normal duties as outlined in your training and the FieldWorks Staff Policies, you now have other additional responsibilities during your work shift. These responsibilities may include but are not limited to:

  • Safely Transporting FieldWorks Employees Assigned by the Director from the Office to Turf
  • Following the FieldWorks Driving Policy
  • Following Driving Directions
  • Dropping Off and Picking Up Canvassers
  • Meeting the Goal Times for First Drop Off Time and Last Attempt Time
  • Handling Mid-Shift Transportation Needs from Team Leader or Director
  • Parking Safely and Legally
  • Following Accident Reporting Guidelines

The work required for this position is to be done only during the normal canvass shift. You are not authorized to work any additional hours as a result of this position with the exception of a one-time initial training for your position of thirty minutes.

Safety is our primary concern for our employees at all times and this is especially true for drivers. We have a zero-tolerance policy for reckless or aggressive driving and require drivers to ensure the safety of everyone in the vehicle.

In order for a FieldWorks employee to be authorized as a driver, employees must agree to the following rules and meet the following conditions:

Requirements to Drive

  • Drivers may not drive on their first day of employment with FieldWorks.
  • Drivers must be age 21 or older.
  • Drivers must have no more than 2 moving violations or accidents in the past 12 months.
  • Drivers must not have had their license suspended or revoked in the last 12 months.
  • Drivers may not have a temporary license or learner’s permit.
  • Drivers may not have a restricted license.
  • Drivers are responsible for transporting any employees assigned to them by the office director.

Rules for Vehicle Operation

  • NO PERSONALLY OWNED VEHICLES MAY BE DRIVEN AT ANY TIME WHILE ON THE CLOCK.
  • No use of cell phones or any handheld devices while operating the vehicle.
  • No drugs or alcohol are to be consumed prior to or during driving a vehicle.
  • No drugs or alcohol are to be consumed within the vehicle, even while parked.
  • Everyone in the vehicle must wear a seatbelt.
  • Accidents on work time, or in a rental vehicle at any time, even very small accidents, must be reported immediately to the office director and the police from the scene of the accident.
  • Driver alone is responsible for any traffic violations or parking citations while operating the vehicle.
  • FieldWorks is not responsible for reimbursing drivers for parking or tolls. Any special agreement for reimbursement of parking or tolls must be made with your office director in advance and you must provide a printed receipt (not handwritten).
