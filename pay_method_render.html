Direct Deposit to Pay Card

Our Pay Card program provides a dependable, safe and very convenient way to receive your pay. You can get your pay through ATM withdrawal, make purchases at stores, and get cash back with those purchases, or even transfer money to a personal or joint checking account. No waiting to get your check, no waiting to cash your check, just use your card to start spending your money. Every employee is eligible for the pay card program. There is no application and no approval process. Any fees for using the card are listed in your enrollment packet.
